About Marina

1) My name is Marina. I am originally from BR, but I have been living in Canada since 2018. After working in the Finance industry for the past years, I realized that I would love to work in an environment where I could use creativity and design to help people and companies. So, in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic, I became more interested in UX design and Graphic Design after taking a few online courses. Recently, I also became very interested in reading books that help me to create good new habits.

I am a curious person who is constantly looking for answers. For this reason, I am always trying to motivate and encourage myself and those around me. I consider myself very organized and detail oriented as my background in finance demanded. Lately, I started to learn more about a few Design Software such as Illustrator and Photoshop.
